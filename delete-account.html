How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Docaro account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below:

1
Open the app and go to Settings
Tap the menu icon or navigate to the Settings section from the main screen.
2
Tap "Delete Account"
Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap "Delete Account and Data".
3
Confirm deletion
You will be asked to confirm your decision. Once confirmed, your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days: • Your account information (email address, authentication data) • All vehicle profiles you created • All service records, including photos of receipts • All reminders and notification settings • All app preferences and settings

What Data May Be Retained

We may retain the following data for legal and compliance purposes: • Anonymized usage analytics (no personal identifiers) — retained for up to 12 months • Transaction records required by financial regulations — retained for up to 7 years as required by law Your subscription history is managed by Apple App Store or Google Play and is subject to their respective data retention policies.

⚠️
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your vehicle history and service records will be lost. We recommend exporting your data (PDF export) before deleting your account.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include the email address associated with your Docaro account. We will process your request within 30 days.
